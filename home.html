Assalamualaikum Everyone!

Welcome to FoodNest. Here, we provide food delivery for all kinds of foods including Malaysian Food and International Food. We have more than 40 branches around Malaysia including Sabah and Sarawak. The money itself will be donated to the needy to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals proposed by the UN.

What is Sustainable Development Goals?

The Sustainable Development Goals or Global Goals are a collection of 17 interlinked global goals designed to be a "blueprint to achieve a better and more sustainable future for all". Our goal is to stop world hunger which aligned with the 2nd goal of the SDG. Join us by donating our cause for the better future of our generation.
